Affirmative
I am…
I am happy · She is tall · They are students
"I am 11 years old. She is my best friend."
Negative
I'm not…
I am not tired · He isn't here · They aren't ready
"I am not sad today. He isn't in the classroom."
Question
Are you…?
Are you ok? → Yes, I am.
Is she here? → No, she isn't.
"Are you ready? — Yes, I am!"
Articles
a / an / the
a dog · an apple · the cat (specific)
"I have a dog. She eats an apple. The dog is big."
Watch out
Use an before a vowel sound: an apple, an elephant, an hour.
Use a before a consonant sound: a dog, a cat, a university.
